SUBJECT: Acquisition Authorities under Portfolio Acquisition Executives


3. Background:


a. On 30 October 2025, the Secretary of the Army signed a memorandum


establishing PAEs, aligned with the Army Warfighting Concepts, empowered to


enhance delivery of capability to the warfighter.


b. Updates to regulations and policies are underway, but it is necessary to publish


immediately the acquisition authorities I choose to delegate or retain.


4. Decisions:


a. I retain MDA or Decision Authority (DA) for all new acquisition programs,


investment activities, and support activities unless delegated in writing via an


Acquisition Decision Memorandum (ADM) or by policy.


b. I will chair Acquisition Shaping Panels and program initiation unless delegated


via ADM or by policy.


c. I retain MDA for all Major Defense Acquisition Programs, regardless of the ACAT


phase.


d. I retain MDA/DA for programs not previously delegated via ADM, to include all


ACAT equivalent programs regardless of pathway.


e. If I approve future delegation of a program, it will be delegated to the appropriate


Capability Portfolio Executive who, by law, possesses the acquisition training,


certifications, and charter to serve in this capacity.


f.


PAEs, regardless of existing acquisition certifications, will not serve as the


MDA/DA.
